About Me

I am Sarkahn (yes, this is my pen name and online presence). A pending (soon to be) short fiction indie author; and this website is a platform used to expose my written wares with the world.

I love story telling. Rekindling my love for fantasy and creation had an incredible effect on me in 2021, and now I wish to listen to these passions whispering in my ear. I am a new writer and would love for others to enjoy reading the stories I put together.

What drew me back into this space was the game Dungeons and Dragons, except from the role of the Dungeon Master. All of the work involved creating a world, characters, plot, setting, experiences pulled me into a void of fiction that I never want to leave again. The experience and process is so much fun I cannot help but talk about it to exhuastive lengths (I am sorry to those of you I bored to death about it).

Due to the pandemic and life in general, nothing is quite the same. With my passion for writing building up, but no way to release through the game due to time constraints, I decided to write about my players, and characters within the world I created. This is only the beginning. I have a lot that I am working on that span across many different literature like literary rpg, romance, and sci-fi.

I feel most comfortable writing short and novelette lengh stories. Though short in length, they allow me to increase the flexibility of the story and provide everyone with a book in their hand much faster than a single novel.

Please look forward to my debut Short "Arcane Thief" coming soon.
