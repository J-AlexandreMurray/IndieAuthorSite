About Me

Sarkahn is a newly published author with a passion for fantasy. Arcane Thief is their debut novelette, published at the end of 2022. Born and raised in the U.S, Sarkahn developed a love of fantasy at a young age.

After completing their studies, they continued to write in their spare time. Arcane Thief is the culmination of stories created within the realm of Sitrah. A world built from the ground up by Sarkahn and partially played through in Dungeons and Dragons as a Game Master.

It tells the story of a young boy who, in his haste and pain, chose to chase after revenge. Since its release, the book has received positive reviews.

In their free time, Sarkahn enjoys world building, computer gaming, and reading about new technologies. They currently live abroad anonymously with their family.

As a new author, Sarkahn is excited to continue writing and exploring their passion for fantasy. Fans of short fiction are sure to enjoy Arcane Thief and should keep and eye out for their short stories presented on their website at sarkahnsvault.com and future works.
